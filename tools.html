Understanding These FPS Tools

What is eDPI?

eDPI (Effective DPI) is the universal sensitivity metric that combines your hardware DPI and in-game sensitivity into a single comparable number. It's how pros compare sensitivity across different hardware setups.

Read our full eDPI guide →

Why CM/360 Matters

CM/360 measures how much physical mousepad distance you need to spin 360° in-game. It's the most accurate way to match muscle memory when switching games or hardware.
