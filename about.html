About Trigger Point Gaming

Trigger Point Gaming was built by a group of competitive FPS players who got tired of sifting through outdated Reddit threads and YouTube videos with vague advice. We wanted one place — clean, fast, free — where a player could find accurate sensitivity calculators, honest gear guides, and genuinely useful performance tips.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: give every competitive player, regardless of budget or experience level, access to the same knowledge and tools that professional esports athletes use. From the basics of what eDPI actually means, to the precise yaw values used in different game engines, we cover the technical side of gaming without dumbing it down.

What We Publish

We focus on four core pillars:

  • Aim & Sensitivity Optimization — Mouse settings, eDPI, grip styles, and everything that affects where your crosshair lands.
  • Gaming Hardware & Gear Guides — Honest, tested recommendations on mice, keyboards, mousepads, and headsets across every budget.
  • System Performance & FPS Tweaks — Windows optimizations, driver settings, and in-game configs that actually move the FPS needle.
  • Display & Monitor Configurations — Refresh rates, response times, monitor settings, and why Hz matters more than most gamers realize.

Our Free Tools

Every tool on this site runs 100% in your browser. No accounts, no downloads, no data collection. Our eDPI calculator, sensitivity converter, 360° distance calculator, and frame time calculator are built with accuracy and speed as the top priority. The underlying conversion multipliers and yaw values are sourced from the FPS community's most trusted technical references.

Editorial Standards

We do not publish sponsored content disguised as editorial. When we recommend a product, it's because we've researched it thoroughly — not because we were paid to. We may earn commissions from affiliate links (disclosed where present), which helps keep the site free and ad-supported.
